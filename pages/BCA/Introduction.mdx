## What is BCA?

The full form of BCA is **Bachelor's in Computer Application**. It is a 3-year undergraduate degree program focused on providing foundational knowledge in computer applications and software development. Equivalent to a BTech/BE in Computer Science or Information Technology, the BCA degree prepares students for advanced careers in Computer Applications. The curriculum covers database management systems, operating systems, software engineering, web technology, and programming languages like C, C++, HTML, and Java.
